Who We Are

A single, trusted partner for your entire workforce ecosystem

Ekaya Consulting is a workforce and business solutions consulting firm specializing in Human Resource solutions, workforce management, recruitment support, payroll administration, compliance management, vendor management, business advisory, and business development consulting.

We work as a strategic partner to businesses — helping them manage the complete employee lifecycle, from workforce planning and hiring to payroll, compliance, workforce management, and employee separation.

Through our extensive network of trusted partners, we provide organizations access to reliable staffing companies, payroll providers, compliance consultants, facility management firms, and other business support service providers.
