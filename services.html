Workforce Management

Payroll, statutory & compliance

Accurate payroll, on-time statutory filings and audit-ready documentation — so your workforce is paid correctly every cycle, and your business stays fully compliant.

10 Services Included

01End-to-End Payroll Processing

We manage the complete payroll cycle from attendance inputs to salary processing and payslip generation, ensuring accuracy, confidentiality and timely disbursement.

02Salary Calculation & Disbursement

Salaries, allowances, deductions, overtime and incentives calculated to your policies, so employees are paid accurately and on time, every cycle.

03Employee Payroll Administration

We maintain payroll records, salary revisions, reimbursements and statutory information to streamline operations and reduce administrative workload.

04Statutory Compliance Support

Support in meeting payroll-related compliance under applicable labour and tax regulations, with proper records, timely filings and audit-ready documentation.

05Provident Fund (PF) Administration

Assistance with PF registration, monthly contributions, employee records and compliance documentation — managed accurately and efficiently.

06Employee State Insurance (ESI) Administration

We manage ESI records, contribution calculations and related compliance so eligible employees receive the benefits provided under the scheme.

07Professional Tax (PT) Compliance

PT calculations, deductions and compliance across applicable states, helping you avoid delays, penalties and reporting errors.

08Tax Deduction at Source (TDS) Processing

Salary TDS calculations, deductions and payroll tax reporting in line with applicable regulations — accurate treatment and full transparency.

09Payroll Reports & MIS

Payroll summaries, cost reports, statutory reports and management dashboards to help leaders monitor workforce costs and payroll trends.

10Employee Payslip Generation

Clear, accurate monthly payslips with complete salary and deduction details, improving payroll transparency and employee communication.
